# Privacy Policy for UpLens

**Last Updated:** July 22, 2026

Welcome to UpLens! Your privacy is incredibly important to us. We built this extension to be 100% open-source and privacy-first. This policy explains what data we handle and how it is protected.

## 1. Information We Collect (None)
UpLens **does not** collect, store, or transmit your personal data, browsing history, or analytics to any of our own servers. We do not maintain a database of our users.

## 2. Local Storage
All data required for the extension to function is stored **locally** on your device using your browser's secure local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This includes:
* **API Keys:** Your OpenAI or Gemini API keys.
* **Your Skills:** The skills you define in the settings or extract from your CV.
* **Analysis History:** The job postings you have analyzed and their AI verdicts.
* **Preferences:** UI settings such as theme and language.

Because this data is stored locally, **we have no access to it.**

## 3. Third-Party Services (AI Providers)
In order to provide AI analysis, UpLens sends the content of the Upwork job posting (Title and Description) and your locally saved "Skills" to the API provider you have selected in your settings (e.g., OpenAI or Google Gemini).
* This data is transmitted directly from your browser to the chosen API provider.
* It does not pass through any intermediate servers owned by us.
* Please review the respective privacy policies of OpenAI or Google regarding how they handle API requests.

## 4. Website Content
The extension only reads the content of the active tab when you are on `upwork.com` and actively request an analysis. It does not monitor or read content from any other websites you visit.

## 5. Contact
